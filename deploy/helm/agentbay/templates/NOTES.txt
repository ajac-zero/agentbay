Prerequisite (not managed by this chart):
  The agent-sandbox CRDs *and* controllers must already be installed in the
  cluster — without the controllers, SandboxClaims will be created but never
  reconciled into Pods, and the orchestrator will hang on every webhook.
  Install them once per cluster (use a tag your platform has tested; the
  e2e suite pins v0.4.6):

    TAG=v0.4.6
    kubectl apply -f https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/agent-sandbox/releases/download/$TAG/manifest.yaml
    kubectl apply -f https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/agent-sandbox/releases/download/$TAG/extensions.yaml

  Verify with:
    kubectl -n agent-sandbox-system get deploy agent-sandbox-controller
